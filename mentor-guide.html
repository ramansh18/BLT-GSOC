OWASP BLT – GSoC Mentor Onboarding Guide

Thank you for your interest in mentoring for OWASP BLT (Bug Logging Tool) under Google Summer of Code (GSoC). Below is the streamlined process to get onboarded.

1

Connect with a Mentee

  • Either a mentee reaches out, or you connect proactively.
  • Align on project scope, expectations, and communication.
2

Join OWASP BLT Slack

  • Join the official BLT Slack workspace.
  • Get added to relevant GSoC channels.
  • Slack is the primary communication medium.
3

Register as a Mentor (GitHub PR)

  • Go to the BLT GSoC repository.
  • Create a PR adding yourself under the Mentors section.
  • Follow the format in this example: github.com/OWASP-BLT/BLT-GSOC/pull/8
  • Wait for review and merge.
4

Create a Dedicated Slack Channel

  • Mentee creates a new Slack channel.
  • Add: mentor, mentee(s), and the project maintainer.
  • This becomes the official working channel.
5

Notify Project Lead

Send a Slack message to the GSoC Admin (message Donnie for more info) including:

  • Confirmation you're onboarding as a mentor.
  • The mentee you're working with.
  • The Slack channel created.
6

Contribute to BLT

Mentors must actively contribute:

  • Submit at least 2–3 minor PRs (bug fixes, improvements, docs).
  • Stay engaged with the codebase.

Quick Checklist

  • Connect with mentee
  • Join Slack
  • Submit mentor PR
  • Create Slack channel
  • Inform project lead
  • Submit 2–3 minor PRs

That's it — simple, contribution-driven onboarding.
